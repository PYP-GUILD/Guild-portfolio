Welcome to PYP Guild

Empowering students through web development and AI-assisted coding

Discover our comprehensive curriculum designed to equip you with the skills needed for a successful tech career.

The Guild program has been developed with the aim to empower the youths of our community with the needed skills for success and enable them to develop the skills required in the ever-changing world of technology and digitalization. It is an extendable 1-year program consisting of an initial 6-month intensive bootcamp followed by 6 months of project-based learning and career support.
